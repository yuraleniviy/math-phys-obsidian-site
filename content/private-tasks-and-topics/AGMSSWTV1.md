## Условие
$$а)\begin{cases} x - y = 3, \\ xy = -2; \end{cases}$$

$$б)\begin{cases} x + y = -1, \\ x^2 + y^2 = 1; \end{cases}$$

$$в)\begin{cases} x + y = 2,5, \\ xy = 1,5; \end{cases}$$

$$г)\begin{cases} x - y = 2, \\ x^2 - y^2 = 17. \end{cases}$$



> [!NOTE]- Ответ
> $$а): (2,−1),(1,−2)$$
> 
> $$б): (0,−1),(−1,0)$$
> 
> $$в): (1.5,1),(1,1.5)$$
> 
> $$г): (5.25,3.25)$$
